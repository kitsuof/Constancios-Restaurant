ABOUT US

Constancio's restaurant was established in March 2021 by Leandra Paras, who named it after her father to honor their strong family roots.

Inspired by her childhood memories in her grandparents' carinderia, Leandra wanted to continue the tradition of bringing people together through food.

The restaurant now serves cozy, home-style dishes made with love and high-quality ingredients, focusing on both flavor and health.

Customers can enjoy their meals at the Sta. Cruz Marvea location in Magalang, Pampanga, or reach out through the website, phone, or Facebook page.
